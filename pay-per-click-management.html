What is PPC Advertising? PPC advertising is a digital marketing model in which advertisers pay a fee each time their ads are clicked. It provides instant visibility on search engines and other online platforms, allowing businesses to reach their target audience precisely. With PPC, you have control over your budget, targeting options, and ad placements, making it a cost-effective and highly measurable advertising method.
